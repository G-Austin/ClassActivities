Brandi Carlile Tribute Page


Bio

Brandi Carlile, born in 1981, has been making music since she dropped out of highschool.

Since then she has released 6 albums with the Hanseroth Twins, seen much success, and founded the Looking Out Foundation, which gives funding to organizations that Brandi is passionate about.

    Released Albums

  1. Brandi Carlile (2005)
  2. The Story (2007)
  3. Give Up the Ghost (2009)
  4. Live at Benaroya Hall with the Seattle Sympony (2011)
  5. Bear Creek (2012)
  6. The Firewatcher's Daughter (2015)
